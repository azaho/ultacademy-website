Our Partners

Co-founding partner

UA-Teens is our co-founding partner and financial sponsor of the program. UA-Teens is also responsible for running the logistics of the program and public relationships of Ukraine Leadership & Technology Academy (ULTA) in Ukrainian media sphere.

UA-Teens covers all expenses for the 20 Ukranian leaders that will be selected to participate in the program. Learn more about funding for ULTA 2023-2024.

About UA-Teens
This section is available only in Ukrainian 🇺🇦

В усіх проєктах, які ми реалізовуємо з 2019 року в освітній сфері, завжди важливу роль посідає соціальна складова та цінності, які лежать в основі ідеї.

Так, відкривши у 2021 році Центр креативних технологій TUMO Kyiv, ми започаткували грантівську програму, за якою на початок повномасштабного вторгнення росії в Україну навчалось 312 тінейджерів безкоштовно. А з квітня 2022 по березень 2023 року 500 українських підлітків з різних куточків України та світу мали можливість здобувати креативні навички в онлайн форматі протягом 9 місяців теж без оплати за своє навчання.

Тому зараз ми раді стати співорганізатором ULTA 2023-2024, роблячи таким чином свій внесок в підготовку проактивної молоді, яка вже через 5 років визначатиме стратегію розвитку нашої держави.

Partners of the final camp

SoftServe is a global IT company with Ukrainian roots, and for over 31 years, it has been a leader in providing digital technology services. More than 13,000 employees have joined the work on 2000+ company projects with clients in the USA, Europe, and APAC. SoftServe also has headquarters in Ukraine and the USA and development centers in Europe and South America, making up a total of 41 offices in 12 countries worldwide.

The NGO “Association Noosphere” is a non-profit organization that has been promoting science, STEM, and space research among children and youth in Ukraine for over 7 years. During these years, more than 56,000 children and teenagers have participated in Noosphere’s space initiatives, over 100,000 in robotics, and more than 25,000 students and young scientists in engineering projects

LVIV OPEN LAB is a STEAM education space from the network TVOR!, where young people can acquire practical skills, implement their ideas, find like-minded individuals, and discover themselves in science. The space includes a chemical laboratory, a tech laboratory, the Creative Workshop “Sverlyk”, a STEAM laboratory, and the Volunteer Center “Idea”. The activities of Lviv Open Lab are aimed at popularizing science and STEAM education, promoting equal opportunities in education, science, and technology, building a scientific community, and supporting the implementation of STEAM educational projects.

CfE Accelerator UCU is an accelerator for Ukrainian startups, with a mission to develop startup culture in Ukraine and increase the competitiveness of Ukrainian startups in the global startup ecosystem. Recently, CfE has also started working with the entrepreneurial sector and created the Stanford Ignite Ukraine program.
